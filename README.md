# Noor Diesel Engineering Company Website

## 📌 Overview

This website is developed for **Noor Diesel Engineering Company**, providing services related to **generator sales, rental, and spare parts purchasing**. The platform is designed to showcase products and services in a clean, professional, and user-friendly way.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 🔧 Generator Services

* Generator **Sales** (new & used)
* Generator **Rental Services** (short-term & long-term)
* Wide range of generator capacities available

### 🛒 Spare Parts Purchasing

* Diesel engine spare parts
* Generator accessories
* High-quality and durable components

### 📦 Product Listings

* Detailed product pages
* Specifications and descriptions
* Pricing (optional / dynamic)

### 📞 Contact & Inquiry

* Easy contact form for customer inquiries
* Business contact details (phone, email, location)
